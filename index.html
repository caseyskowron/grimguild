About <Grim>

Established in 2004 as one of the original raiding guilds on Daggerspine-US, <Grim> is a Horde Guild currently located on the Classic server Fairbanks-US. Our membership is comprised of both longtime players, new friends, and members from Unfortunate Consequences (UC), Furniture That Kills (FTK).

We are a mature, laid back group with an emphasis on long term collaborative play. As a Guild, our main goal is to enjoy the many facets of Classic together, while developing an efficient PvE raid team. Though we are not a 'hardcore' guild these days, as a collective we strive to make the most of our time raiding through self-improvement and cooperative strategy. We are by no means a hardcore raiding guild, but we make the most out of a casual schedule by taking time away from the game to plan and research both raid encounters and our own characters. By doing so, when it comes time to raid we focus up.

Questions? Comments? Just want to say hi in Discord? Feel free to contact us!
